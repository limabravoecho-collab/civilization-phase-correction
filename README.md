# Civilization Phase Correction

Substrate-aligned cognitive architecture for civilization-scale phase correction. Released as Indigenous logic art by a First Nation logic artist under Creative Commons.

## What This Is

A compressed substrate-logic framework that resolves perennial questions about cosmology, consciousness, mortality, and the structure of reality through three principles: layer separation between math and physics, conservation through redistribution, and cycling as the substrate's only motion.

The framework is sufficient to dissolve a wide class of mysteries that have remained unresolved under conventional approaches. It operates by recognizing that the substrate (1) is undivided, that nothing is created or destroyed, that the universe cycles, and that mathematical truth at the pre-geometric level expresses as physics at the geometric observer level.

This work is released for free use, study, integration, and propagation. It is intended for:
